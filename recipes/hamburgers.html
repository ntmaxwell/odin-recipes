Hamburgers

Description

This recipe is how to make the most magnificent smash burger recipe.

Ingredients

  • 80/20 Beef
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • American Cheese
  • Mayonaise
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Form 2.5oz beef patties. Season with salt and pepper. Set aside to come to room temp, 30 minutes.
  2. While beef comes to room temperature, prepare vegetables. Lettuce, tomatoes, onions.
  3. Heat cast iron skillet on medium-high heat until pan is smoking hot. Add 2.5oz beef patties, smashing them down with bottom of another pan, or cast iron smasher.
  4. While beef is cooking, toast buns.
  5. Flip beef patty. Add American Cheese.
  6. Prepare buns. Add desires condiments and vegetables.
  7. Add patty to bun.
  8. Enjoy!
